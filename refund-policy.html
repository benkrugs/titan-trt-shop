Refund & Return Policy

Important: Due to the medical nature of our at-home test kits, all sales are final once the kit has been shipped. We cannot accept returns of used or opened test kits for health and safety reasons.

Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. However, due to health regulations and product safety, returns are limited to specific circumstances:

Eligible for Return:

  • Unopened Kits: Unused test kits in original, sealed packaging may be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Damaged Products: Items damaged during shipping (must be reported within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Wrong Item Shipped: If you received an incorrect product (must be reported within 7 days of delivery)
  • Defective Products: Manufacturing defects or missing components (must be reported within 7 days of delivery)

NOT Eligible for Return:

  • Opened or used test kits
  • Kits with broken seals or packaging
  • Items returned after 14 days from delivery date
  • Custom or personalized orders

Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us
Email support@titan-trt.com with your order number, reason for return, and photos if applicable (for damaged items). Our team will respond within 24 hours.

Step 2: Receive Authorization
If your return is approved, we will provide a Return Authorization (RA) number and return shipping instructions.

Step 3: Ship Item Back
Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship to the address provided. Include your RA number on the package.

Step 4: Refund Processing
Once we receive and inspect your return (2-3 business days), we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Return Shipping Costs

  • Defective/Damaged Items: SSK Marketing LLC pays return shipping
  • Wrong Item Shipped: SSK Marketing LLC pays return shipping
  • Customer Remorse: Customer pays return shipping (deducted from refund)

Refund Methods & Timeframes

Refund Amount:

  • Full purchase price for eligible returns
  • Original shipping costs refunded only if item was defective/damaged or wrong item shipped
  • Return shipping costs (if applicable) deducted from refund

Refund Timeframe:

  • 2-3 business days for return inspection
  • 5-7 business days for refund processing
  • 3-10 business days for funds to appear in your account (depending on your bank)
  • Total estimated time: 10-20 business days

Refund Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your test kit arrives damaged or defective:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@titan-trt.com
  2. Provide photos of the damaged packaging and/or product
  3. Include your order number in the email

We will send a replacement kit at no cost or issue a full refund (your choice).

Cancellations

Before Shipment: Orders can be cancelled for a full refund if processing has not yet begun. Contact us immediately at support@titan-trt.com.

After Shipment: Once your order has shipped, standard return policies apply. You may refuse delivery and contact us for a refund (subject to return shipping fees).

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different product, please return your original order (if eligible) and place a new order.
